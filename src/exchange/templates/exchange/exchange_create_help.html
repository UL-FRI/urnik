Help

Students

  • Choose the target lab cycles you want from the dropdowns.
  • You can request a specific student to exchange with you by using their enrollment number (63xxxxxx) or their LDAP identity (xy1234@student.uni-lj.si)
  • Exchanges may be fulfilled immediately or placed into a queue. You can cancel any pending exchange you initiated.
  • Requesting multiple exchanges at once does not create a set - they are fulfilled individually. However, requesting a specific student applies to all choices.

Teachers

  • In your subject overview, you may disable exchanges entirely for a particular subject, or set a date up to which exchanges are allowed.
  • It is possible to offer a lab cycle switch to a particular student, even if there is not enough room in the lab cycle. The student must then accept this proposal. The order in which a teacher's and a student's requests are placed does not matter.
